Symptoms

  • Print jobs stuck in “Queued” or “Deleting”.
  • Restarting the app doesn’t help.

Quick Fix (Windows)

  1. Press Win + R, type services.msc, press Enter.
  2. Find Print Spooler → Right‑click → Restart.
  3. Clear the queue folder: open %systemroot%\\System32\\spool\\PRINTERS and delete files.

One‑liner (Run as Admin)

net stop spooler & del /q %systemroot%\System32\spool\PRINTERS\* & net start spooler

Tip: If it keeps happening, update the printer driver and check port settings.
